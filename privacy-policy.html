Introduction

AR Development (operated by Roger, trading as atRoger) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • The content of your message

We do not collect any information automatically beyond what your browser transmits as part of a standard HTTP request (such as your IP address or browser type), which is handled by our hosting provider and not stored or processed by us.

How We Use Your Information

The information you submit through the contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or project quote request
  • Follow up about work we have discussed

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use Web3Forms to process contact form submissions. When you submit the form, your name, email address, phone number, and message are transmitted to Web3Forms' servers and forwarded to our business email inbox. Web3Forms processes this data solely to deliver your message and does not retain it for their own use. You can review the Web3Forms Privacy Policy on their website.

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising pixels, or any other third-party tracking services on this site.

Cookies and Local Storage

This website does not use cookies. We use a single browser localStorage entry (key: theme) to remember your preferred color theme between visits. This preference is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are received in our business email account and retained for as long as necessary to fulfill your request or for legitimate business record-keeping. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

Your Rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Correction of any inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your personal data from our records

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at roger@atRoger.com.

External Links

This site may contain links to third-party websites such as social media profiles. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the website following an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
